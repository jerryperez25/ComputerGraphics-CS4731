Please use a browser that supports the "canvas" tag.

Press X to translate your wireframe in the positive x direction.

Press C to translate your wireframe in the negative x direction.

Press Y to translate your wireframe in the positive y direction.

Press U to translate your wireframe in the negative y direction.

Press Z to translate your wireframe in the positive z direction.

Press A to translate your wireframe in the negative z direction.

Press R to rotate your wireframe in an X-roll about it's current position.

Press B to toggle mesh pulsing.

Press each key once more to stop the action.
